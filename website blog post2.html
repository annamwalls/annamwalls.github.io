5 TIPS TO KEEPING YOUR WEEKLY GROCERIES UNDER $100

1. PLAN YOUR MEALS BEFORE YOU GO TO THE SUPERMARKET.

WRITE WHAT INGREDIENTS YOU NEED IN YOUR SHOPPING LIST. THIS WILL SAVE UNNECESSARY FOOD BUYING AND OVER SPENDING


2. BUY YOUR FRUIT AND VEGE AT A LOCAL FARMERS MARKET!

THIS WILL SAVE YOU SOME MONEY AND ALSO YOU KNOW YOU ARE GETTING FAIRLY ORAGANIC PRODUCE!


3.LOOK FOR THOSE DEALS!

WHEN BUYING ITEMS ALWAYS CHECK IF YOU CAN GET IT CHEAPER WITH A DIFFERENT BRAND- YOU NEVER KNOW IT MIGHT EVEN END UO BEING HEALTHIER!


4. ALTERNATE BETWEEN THE MORE EXPENSIVE ITEMS.

A PRACTICE I DO IS TO CHOOSE BETWEEN TWO THINGS I ENJOY FROM THE SAME FOOD FAMILY GROUP AND BUY THEM ON ALTERNATIVE WEEKS. FOR EXAMPLE IN A WEEK I NEED TO BUY FROZEN FRUIT I WON'T BUY GREEK YOGURT.


5. LASTLY MAKE ROOM FOR YOUR MUST HAVE ITEMS!

DON'T DEPRIVE YOURSELF FROM AN ITEM THAT YOU LOVE BECAUSE OF THE PRICE. FIND A WEEK THAT WILL SUIT YOU TO GET IT AND WRITE IT IN YOUR LIST, ITS OKAY TO TREAT YOURSELF SOMETIMES!
